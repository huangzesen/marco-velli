# Marco Velli — Profile

## Overview

Marco Velli is an Italian-American solar physicist, professor at UCLA, 
and the Observatory Scientist for NASA's Parker Solar Probe mission. 
His career spans four decades of contributions to our understanding of 
the solar wind, Alfvén waves, coronal heating, and magnetic reconnection.
